About

Max is currently living and working in Tāmaki Makaurau. Having recently completed a Bachelor of Honours Degree in Visual Communication Design from Massey University in Pōneke, Wellington, he is now working as a graphic designer at Haumi.

Previous to that Max spent two years working at Scratch with a particular focus in defining, designing and delivering commercial work to clients across construction, hospitality and professional services.

Max has an honest but direct approach to design and is interested in intersecting this with a focus on his Mātauranga Māori. He now has a particular focus in interactive and printed media as mediums to communicate visual themes and ideas through his work.
