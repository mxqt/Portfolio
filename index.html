He Kaiwhakatauira Whakanikoniko e noho ana ki Extended Whānau.


Max is a graphic designer working at the intersection of contemporary design and mātauranga Māori. After completing his undergraduate studies in 2020 at Toi Rauwhārangi, Massey University, Max has been based in Tāmaki Makaurau, working across a range of kaupapa to create work grounded in tikanga, story, identity, and the communities it serves.
